Primary report

City budgets return to discipline as refinancing windows become usable again.

Public finance teams are acting with greater precision rather than greater confidence. The result is a quieter but more credible capital plan, where timing, sequence, and project legitimacy matter more than optimistic assumptions about the rate path.

What changed

Stability in debt markets created room for agencies to move, but political support packages became narrower at the same time.

Why it matters

Readers get a more actionable story when financing, policy scope, and delivery risk are placed in one frame.
